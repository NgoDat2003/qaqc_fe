# Design System: MayCha QA/QC Premium Identity
**Project ID:** MAYCHA-QAQC-FE

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**Atmosphere:** "Sophisticated, Precise, and Trustworthy."
The design shifts from generic SaaS blue to a specialized **Imperial Gold & Deep Charcoal** aesthetic. It uses high contrast to emphasize clarity and precision, reflecting the nature of quality auditing. The interface feels "Airy" yet "Solid" through the use of generous whitespace and sharp, defined borders.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Imperial Gold (#D4AF37):** Primary Action color. Used for buttons, active states, and key icons. Conveys premium quality and excellence.
*   **Deep Charcoal (#0F172A):** Main background for the brand panel. Provides a professional, stable foundation.
*   **Pearl White (#F8FAFC):** Main background for the interaction area. Ensures maximum readability.
*   **Glow Amber (#F59E0B):** Used for subtle shadows and focus rings to add "life" to the UI.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Font Family:** Inter (via Google Fonts).
*   **Headers:** Semibold with tight line-height and `tracking-tight` (-0.025em) for a modern, compact look.
*   **Body:** Medium weight for labels to ensure accessibility against white/dark backgrounds.
*   **Accents:** All-caps with `tracking-widest` (0.1em) for category labels (e.g., "F&B QUALITY MANAGEMENT").

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:** Gently rounded (8px), solid Gold background with white/charcoal text. On hover, a subtle amber outer glow.
*   **Cards/Containers:** "Glassmorphism" for the login form when on mobile/overlay. Whisper-soft diffused shadows (`shadow-xl` with reduced opacity).
*   **Inputs/Forms:** Transparent background with a thin `1px` border. On focus, the border turns Gold with a soft amber glow.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Split Ratio:** 45/55 split for Desktop to give the brand imagery enough room to breathe.
*   **Whitespace:** Consistent `p-10` (40px) padding for all major containers.
*   **Responsiveness:** Fluid transition to a single-column layout on Mobile, with the brand logo centered at the top.
